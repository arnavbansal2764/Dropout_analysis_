Aim

This Projects aims at providing probability of student dropping in a state. Provided the data contains the information of school name, area (urban, rural or suburban), gender, caste, age, dropout(YES/NO). This Project also gives the pictorial representation of the students dropping vs the parameters mentioned above.We do a comprehensive analysis of student dropout patterns, taking into account various categories, and use the insights gained to inform policy decisions.This will help us identify high risk area and demographics and pinpoint schools, areas, and demographics where dropout rates are notably high. This includes identifying regions with the highest poverty rates, gender disparities, caste-based discrimination, and age-specific challenges.

The analysis of student dropouts for school education can lead to several long-term benefits and avenues for further exploration:

  • Early Warning Systems
  • Customized Educational Support
  • Research and Policy Impact Assesment
